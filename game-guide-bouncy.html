Getting Started / FAQ

How do I play the game?
Press the space bar to move and see how far you can go without crashing the bouncy ball into the tubes!

How do I check my version number?
Press on the Mad Lemons button in the top right corner (it's the Mad Lemons Logo) and it will say on the bottom right.

How do I play without keyboard?
No keyboard, no problem! Press on the circle in the bottom right corner to bounce the ball.

Obstacles

There are several types of obstacles in the game.

The regular tubes do not move and stay still. They can either be attached to only one side or both.
Moving Tubes can move around vertically.
Expanding tubes expand and contract vertically.

How many obstacles are in the map?

  • Regular Tubes (Top and Bottom): 1
  • Regular Tubes: 8
  • Moving Tubes: 2
  • Expanding Tubes: 4
