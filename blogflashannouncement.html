IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT: Regarding the future of our games...

Posted on: Friday, May 1st, 2020

Since 2015, Mad Lemons has made a varity of web games - over a varity of different topics and generes. Our games have been played by millions and updated regularly for years. Our team loves working on our games, and has for years, and we plan to continue to in the future. However, there is a time where technology advances, and our studio and games must keep up with an ever changing world, and sometimes, the best games must come to an end.

If you have not heard, Adobe Flash Player will be removed from all devices beginning on December 31st, 2020. All Mad Lemons games are written in this coding language. In the upcoming months, we will be working on converting our portfolio of active games to the new language.

On top of flash, our games are coded with two different engines, Cyberix3D, and Sploder. We have made the difficult decision to discontinue our Sploder games effective on December 31st, 2020. We will also no longer be supporting these games with updates and bug fixes beginning today. The game engine has not been updated in several years and there are no plans to update it HTML5 or another language from Flash Player. A complete rewrite from scratch of these games would be required. with a different engine. (These games are written in 2D, unlike our other games. which are written in a 3D environment)

 

To reitterate, we will be ending support on the following games effective as of today, May 1st, 2020. They will still be able to be played until they are discontinued on December 31st, 2020.

  • Jumping Mania (2016)
  • Destroyer Mania (2016)
  • Spaceship Mania (2016)
  • Jumping Mania 2 (2016)
  • Robot Mania (2016)
  • Jumping Mania: Year 2100 (2017)

 

Our other games, listed below, will continue to recieve updates throughout the rest of 2020, expect no change to our content schedule. Behind the scenes, we will be converting all of our games to another programming language by the end of the year. Once the port is complete, instead of playing inside the desktop browser, you will need to install a seperate Mad Lemons desktop client. There will only be one installation needed for all games. All games will continue to update automatically. Our website will continue to serve as a hub for all our games.

  • Casino Fever (2015)
  • Bouncy (2015)
  • Racing Through Space (2016)
  • Ball Bounce Challenge! (2018)

 

Of the list above, only Ball Bounce Challenge! uses our new saving method. This is behind the scenes. It is almost impossible to convert data from the old engine to the new engine, so every previous user will need to contact support to transfer their skins, with their Mad Lemons ID as proof. We will still be able to check even after the old game is gone. As of now, there is only a few customizations since Ball Bounce Challenge v2.0.0. We will continue to release new skins and customizations over the rest of the year.

Thank you everyone and have fun. If you have any questions contact support or leave a message on our forums!
